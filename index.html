About the fund
Senior Class Impact Fund
Designed by the Class of 2026 Giving Committee, this new fund will support graduating seniors facing financial pressure as they take their next steps after Pitzer. Alleviating financial burdens will remove obstacles and increase access to resources, experiences, and opportunities for all seniors — setting them up for success and propelling them forward on their future paths.
What it supports
Grad school application fees
Interview travel costs
Fellowship programs
Thesis materials
Professional networks
Access to opportunities
